Our Cubs went down 0-2 to Haiti in the #YOG football semi-final

admin

August 22, 2010

It just wasn’t our night. Our boys couldn’t get into their usual rhythm. In the first half, and conceded a goal. They came out fighting in the second half, and had several chances, but no luck.

When we conceded a penalty and were reduced to 10 men, it was over. I told the cubs we were all proud of them, and will stand by them. Next match on Wednesday 25 August 2010.
