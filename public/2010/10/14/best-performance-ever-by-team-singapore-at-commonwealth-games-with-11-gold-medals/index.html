Best performance ever by Team Singapore at Commonwealth Games with 11 Gold medals

admin

October 14, 2010

Our team will bring home 11 Gold, 11 Silver and 9 Bronze medals.

For comparison, during the period from 1958 to 2006, Singapore won 13 Gold medals.

We are on the right track. Congratulations to all the athletes, parents, officials and supporters!
