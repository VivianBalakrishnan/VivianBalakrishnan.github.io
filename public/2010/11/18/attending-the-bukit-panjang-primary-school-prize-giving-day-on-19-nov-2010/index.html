Attending the Bukit Panjang Primary School Prize Giving Day on 19 Nov 2010

admin

November 18, 2010

Bukit Panjang Primary School will always be special for me. Both my parents were teachers in the school during the 1950s. In fact, that’s where they met and fell in love. So in a sense, I owe my existence to this school.

Even today, many people still come up to tell me that my parents taught them so many years ago.
