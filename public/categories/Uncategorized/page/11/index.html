November 11, 2010

12 suspects arrested so far in connection with the slashings at Bukit Panjang

Police telephoned me earlier this evening to inform me that they have arrested 12 young men so far.

The Criminal Law (Temporary Provisions) Act provides for detention without trial in order to facilitate thorough investigations into cases like this. I am glad we still have these laws and an effective Police Force capable of quick and decisive action to break the backs of gangs like this.

Don’t hesitate to call the Poice if you notice any suspicious people loitering in our neighbourhood.

Many generations of eye care professionals and patients will owe him so much.

October 9, 2010

Jasmine Ser and Cheng Jian Huan win Silver in the 10m Air Rifle (Pair) ; Gai Bin and Poh Lip Meng take Bronze in 25m Pistol (Pairs) in Commonwealth Games

Jasmine and Jian Huan saw an early lead erode as the Malaysian pair overtook them in the latter stage with a 793 to 790 point victory. I had a chance to chat with both of them before presenting them with scarves at the Victory Ceremony. Jasmine developed dry eyes mid way, and despite eye drops just couldn’t clear her eyes well enough. Poor Jian Huan was nursing a flu, and fighting drowsiness. Nevertheless, it was an excellent performance, and they have another day of competition tomorrow.
