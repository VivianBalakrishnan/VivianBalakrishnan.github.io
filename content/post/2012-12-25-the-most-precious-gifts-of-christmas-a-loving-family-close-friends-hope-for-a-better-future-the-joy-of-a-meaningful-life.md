<p>The combination of a festive air and basic challenges of life gave us food for thought as we shared a meal after the session.</p>

<p>The most valuable blessings we have are a loving family, close friends, the hope for a better future and a meaningful life. Everything else is secondary. Happiness is not a ledger of possessions, events and achievements.
